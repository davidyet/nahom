    Zoos are very important and should be kept no matter what. There are so many pros to the existence of Zoos. For example, because of the uneducated thinking that rhino horn is a medicine, poachers are paid big money to be able to kill rhino’s so that they could take the horns and grind them up to a powder to be sold for hundreds and thousands of dollars. Use to living in South Africa, I can personally attest to the fact that we would always see the statistics on how many rhinos are being killed. Seeing all those pictures of a dead rhino or elephant lying dead on the ground with a circular-cut on their nose where the horn used to be sickness me. With a zoo, you can create a habitat where rhinos can roam around freely with the ability to reproduce and be kept safe from rhino poachers. Not just rhinos and elephants but all endangered animals can be held in a habitat where they are safe. Not just for safety but instead of having to go to an indigenous area to see a special animal like a tiger or cheetah you can go to your local zoo. We could do research on different animals without having to go to any dangerous areas. Zoos can also be a source of economic income for the area that it’s in because it brings many visitors to that community
